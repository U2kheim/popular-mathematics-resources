IMPACT Popular Mathematics Resource Library

Welcome to the IMPACT Popular Mathematics Resource Library. This library consists of more than 400 sources of mathematically oriented popular science to serve as inspiration for teachers, students and others interested in learning more about the history, culture and composition of mathematics. Explore the filters to find a resource that fits your needs. Enquiries and contributions can be directed towards ivan.tokheim@hvl.no
